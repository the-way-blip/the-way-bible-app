## Description (4000 chars max)

The Way is a complete KJV Bible study app designed to help you read, understand, and memorize Scripture.

Tap any word while reading to instantly see its original Greek or Hebrew meaning from Strong's Concordance. Highlight verses in multiple colors, add personal notes, and build a collection of memory verses with five interactive practice modes.

STUDY THE ORIGINAL LANGUAGES
Every word in the KJV is linked to Strong's Concordance with Greek and Hebrew definitions, transliterations, pronunciations, etymology, and usage data. Tap a word to go deeper. Access additional resources from BibleHub, Blue Letter Bible, and Webster's 1828 Dictionary.

MEMORIZE SCRIPTURE
Save verses and practice with spaced repetition. Choose from five modes: Flashcard, First Letter hints, Fill-in-the-Blank, Type It, and Word Ordering. The app tracks your progress and schedules reviews so verses move to long-term memory.

HIGHLIGHT & TAKE NOTES
Mark up Scripture with four highlight colors. Add personal notes to any verse. Everything stays organized and syncs across your devices when you create an account.

JOURNAL YOUR FAITH
Write dated journal entries linked to specific passages. Track your mood and revisit what God has been teaching you over time.

PRAYER LIST
Keep a running list of prayer requests. Mark prayers as answered and look back to see God's faithfulness.

EXPLORE 60+ TOPICS
Browse curated verse collections on Salvation, Faith, Love, Wisdom, Prayer, Hope, Forgiveness, Spiritual Warfare, and more.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
See how many chapters you've read, maintain a daily reading streak, and earn achievement badges as you grow in the Word.

PERSONALIZED EXPERIENCE
- Adjustable font size and six font options
- Light and dark themes
- Read Mode for flowing text, Study Mode for word-by-word analysis
- Verse numbers on or off
- Swipe to navigate chapters

SEARCH THE WHOLE BIBLE
Find any verse by keyword or reference. Search history and suggested topics make it easy to find what you're looking for.

VERSE OF THE DAY
Start each day with a curated verse from Scripture. Copy or share it with friends and family.

SYNC ACROSS DEVICES
Create a free account to back up your highlights, notes, memory verses, journal entries, and reading progress to the cloud.

Read the Bible. Follow Jesus.
